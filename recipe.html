How to Make Halo-Halo

Instructions

  1. Use your shaved ice maker to make shaved ice
  2. In a serving glass, layer sweet red bean, sweet white beans, coconut gel, macapuno, and jackfruit.
  3. Top with shaved ice.
  4. Drizzle about one tablespoon of evaporated milk over shaved ice.
  5. Top with a scoop of ube ice cream and slices of leche flan. Enjoy immediately!

Tools and Equipment

  • Serving Glass
  • Measuring spoons
  • Shaved Ice Maker (optional)

Ingredients

  • Sweet red bean (munggo)
  • Sweet white beans
  • Coconut gel (nata de coco)
  • Macapuno (gelatinous coconut string)
  • Fresh or canned jackfruit, cut into chunks
  • Shaved ice
  • Leche Flan
  • Evaporated milk
  • Ube ice cream
